DM Travel Buddy

Resolve a day of travel with terrain, danger, pace, player rolls, travel outcomes, and possible events.

Player Roll Driven Travel Modifiers Outcome Events

How to Use

Choose the terrain, danger level, and travel pace. Have a player roll a travel check, then enter the total. The app adds modifiers and resolves the travel outcome.

Outcome Tiers

  • 1–5: Disaster
  • 6–10: Hardship
  • 11–15: Quiet Travel
  • 16–20: Opportunity
  • 21–25: Windfall
  • 26+: Legendary Fortune

Travel Pace Modifiers

  • Careful: +2 to the travel score
  • Normal: +0 to the travel score
  • Fast: -2 to the travel score

Danger Modifiers

  • Safe: +4 to the travel score
  • Tense: +1 to the travel score
  • Strange: -1 to the travel score
  • Dangerous: -3 to the travel score

Event Results

  • Bad results: Penalties, danger, delays, or difficult choices.
  • Quiet Travel: No event appears; the day is mostly defined by progress and conditions.
  • Good results: Benefits, shortcuts, clues, safe rest, or rare fortune.

Enter the player's travel check total before modifiers.
