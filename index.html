Travel / Status Console

DM Travel Buddy

Resolve a day of overland travel with terrain, danger, pace, player rolls, travel outcomes, and possible events.

Resolve Travel How It Works
Current Mode

Outcome Resolver

Player roll plus travel modifiers determines whether the journey becomes a disaster, hardship, quiet day, opportunity, windfall, or legendary fortune.

Procedure

How to Use

Choose the travel conditions, enter the player’s travel check total, and let the app resolve the outcome.

Outcome Tiers

  • 1–5: Disaster
  • 6–10: Hardship
  • 11–15: Quiet Travel
  • 16–20: Opportunity
  • 21–25: Windfall
  • 26+: Legendary Fortune

Travel Pace

  • Careful: +2 to travel score
  • Normal: +0 to travel score
  • Fast: -2 to travel score

Danger Level

  • Safe: +4 to travel score
  • Tense: +1 to travel score
  • Strange: -1 to travel score
  • Dangerous: -3 to travel score

Event Results

  • Bad results: Penalties, danger, delays, or hard choices.
  • Quiet Travel: No event card appears.
  • Good results: Benefits, shortcuts, clues, or rare fortune.

Input

Travel Console

Set the conditions for today’s journey.

Arcane Route Check

Enter the player’s travel check total before modifiers.
